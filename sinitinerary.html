Getting Around Singapore

  • More than 130 stations across six MRT lines span the island. This 200km system has over three million daily ridership.

  • Shuttle Bus Services are also operated to cater to specific transport needs, such as connecting to places of interest, tourist attractions, commercial or retail centres and medical institutions.

  • Taxis or private hire cars offer all-hours convenience and door-to-door comfort. In Singapore, you can flag a taxi along the street or at designated taxi stands, or book a ride anytime via mobile apps.

  • Weaving into the heartlands, business hubs and tourist attractions, buses bring you closer to your destination.
